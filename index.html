Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is BUY iT?

BUY iT is an online space that connects sellers and buyers quickly through WhatsApp. It’s fast, simple, and easy to use

How does BUY iT work?

Sellers submit their products, and buyers browse those products through our landing page. When a buyer clicks on a product, they're taken directly to the WhatsApp group or seller's DM

Is it free to join BUY iT?

Yes! Both sellers and buyers can join for free during this early phase

How do I join as a seller?

Click the "Start selling" button, and you'll be added to the appropriatee WhatsApp group to start posting your products.

How do I buy something?

Browse the product listings. When you find what you like, click it, and you'll be redirected to the seller or the right WhatsApp group to chat and buy.

What kind of products can be listed?

We accept legal and safe product product-fashion, food, gadgets, beauty, and more. Product like cars, house, and land are not to be sold on BUY iT. Harmful or fake items are not allowed.

What hapens if a product is not as described?

Report the seller to us. We take these things seriously and may remove sellers who are dishonest or suspicious.

Can I invite others to join BUY iT?

Yes! You're welcome to invite more sellers or buyers using the share link.

When will the official BUY iT app launch?

We're working on it! Stay active in the community-early members will get special benefits when we go live.
